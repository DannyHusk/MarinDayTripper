Why exactly, have me walk your dog?

forget all that though!

my 'big selling point is' - I walk dogs out in nature only

- dog walking -

- about me -

My name is Tim Scott.



I grew up in the rocky mountains of Wyoming. I have very outdoorsy parents who always encouraged me to keep drawing in life. I was constantly drawing, camping and swimming, running and watching the NBA growing up. 

Music is a huge part of my life. the beach boys, the Beatles, Queen, CCR, Nirvana, Madness, Cypress Hill. There really isn't any music i dislike. I called my dog walking service 'day tripper' after the Beatles song.

I worked as lead artist (sculptor) in VFX and Video Games for the last 20 years. I worked for Disney, Marvel, Zynga, Trion Worlds etc, etc. Being an artist pays the bills but it's never really been my passion.
My passion in life is my family, cooking and baking, playing guitar, sculpting, nature and THE WARRIORS!!!

I've wanted to walk dogs for the last 10 or so years now. My last job in video games i resigned.
I've never quit a job but i felt it was time to start being happy in life, doing something i really care about.

I love people, animals and nature.
I want to help dog owners give their dogs an excellent life.
